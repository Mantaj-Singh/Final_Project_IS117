Team Composition

Making a team can seem like a daunting task at first. There are so many Pokemon in the game that seem like viable options to put on your team. First, we must take a step back. Think about a Pokemon you want to use. It doesn't matter whether or not they are considered good or bad. Once you have something in mind, try making a team around it. Is the Pokemon you chose a defensive one, or is it more of a glass cannon? If the latter, maybe you want to make a hyper offensive team.
