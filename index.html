This resource is a great way to experiment and visualize your team. This easy to understand site can help you figure out what abilities you want to use, what moves you can choose from, and how to structure your EV's and IV's

Pikalytics

This resource is a great help in determining what to bring to a VGC competition. Pikalytics provides all sorts of data for players to see what is being used in the meta.
