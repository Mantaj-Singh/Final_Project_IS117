What are EV's and IV's?

EV's are called effort value, while IV's are called individual values. IV's are determined the moment you either catch or hatch a Pokemon. EV's on the other hand are values obtained by fighting wild Pokemon. Both determine the stat of a Pokemon. For example, if a Pokemon has a high base HP stat, it is common to invest as many EV's as possible to HP in order to maximize the stat. Maximizing a stat is what EV's are for, IV's almost always want to be as high as possible. You can have a total of 31 IV's for every stat, but you can only maximize two stats with EV's.
