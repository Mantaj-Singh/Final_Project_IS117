Strategies During the Game

There are many matchups in Pokemon that may seem unfavorable towards your team. However, fret not! Every team will have to go through a difficult matchup. Teams that are common in today's meta are not as weather oriented as usual with the exception of sun teams. This is due to how changing the weather is now possible by almost every pokemon on the field, depending on who you choose to dynamax. Sun teams are still very common, though, due to the use of gigantimax Venusaur and the deadly effects of G-Max Vinelash.

A big threat to watch out for is the grass starter: Rillaboom. Due to the combination of it's hidden ability grassy surge- which puts up a grassy terrain and a move called grassy glide- which gives it a strong priority option- this Pokemon is difficult to build around.
